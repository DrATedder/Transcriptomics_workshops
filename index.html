Transcriptomics Module: Learning Materials

Welcome! This landing page provides links to all course materials, reading, and practical activities. Please complete items in order unless otherwise advised. To check how much you've understood as you progress through, there are a number of short quizzes and 'critical thinking' exercises (essentially, data interpretation). This short course aims to get you thinking about transcriptomic data and analysis before you start a project of your own. That might mean understanding what data in your subject area is out there, and using it to imrove your study design; or it might mean getting to grips with key stages in the process. These workshops critically do not require any familiarity with coding, and should run on any laptop or computer.
